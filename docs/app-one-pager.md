# In the Park: Updated One-Pager

## What It Is
In the Park is a dog-owner app built around real everyday routines: finding parks, tracking walks, managing pet records, and discovering trusted local dog-friendly services and community.

The clearest way to position it today is:

**In the Park helps dog owners get outside more, stay organized, and connect with local dog life in one app.**

## The Core Promise
Dog owners should not need one app for walks, another for pet records, another for park discovery, and another for local pet community.

In the Park brings those moments together:
- Search for parks by name
- Save liked parks for quick return visits
- Discover dog-owner news and tips in the app
- Check in at parks and see live activity
- Track walks, routes, time, and progress
- Build pet profiles and manage records
- Join chats, groups, and direct messages
- Find nearby pet services

## Product Functionality
### 1. Park search and park discovery
- Search parks by name and sort results by distance
- Like favorite parks for fast access later
- Identify dog parks and off-leash parks
- View active user counts and park activity
- Open park chat from park cards

### 2. For You experience
- Search park at the top of the tab
- See liked parks in one place
- Browse fresh dog-owner news and tips from sources like Reddit, AKC, PetMD, and Dogster
- Keep the app feeling active even when local user density is still growing

### 3. Walk tracking
- Start and end walks in-app
- Track route, duration, and movement
- Save walk history
- Add walk notes and revisit past activity
- Build streaks and progression around outdoor routines

### 4. Pet profiles and records
- Create profiles for multiple dogs
- Save dog details beyond name and breed, including sex, size, weight, birthday, and temperament
- Store pet documents with expiry dates
- Support vaccine, licence, microchip, medical, insurance, and custom document types
- Open stored documents through owner-only access instead of public links

### 5. Social and communication
- Park chat tied to real places
- Group chat for organized communities
- Direct messaging between users
- Group unread indicators and notifications when new group messages arrive

### 6. Groups and local coordination
- Create public or private groups
- Set meetup location and time
- Manage group details
- Join conversations with local dog owners

### 7. Local pet business discovery
- Browse pet services in the same app environment where users already search parks and manage dog life
- Useful for groomers, walkers, trainers, daycare, rescues, and local pet operators

### 8. Motivation and retention
- XP and levels
- Streaks and progress systems
- Walk-centered engagement loop that rewards active use

## Why It Matters
### For dog owners
- Makes dog life simpler and more organized
- Gives users practical value even before a large social network forms
- Combines utility and community in one place
- Helps pet parents keep records, walks, parks, and discovery together

### For pet businesses

### For expo audiences

- Easy to demo live

## Best Expo Positioning
Do not lead with “social network.”

Lead with:

**Find parks, track walks, manage pet records, and discover local dog life in one app.**

That message is stronger because it works for:
- solo utility users
- active dog park users
- new puppy parents
- multi-dog households
- local pet businesses

## Suggested Demo Flow
1. Show the `For You` tab with park search, liked parks, and dog news.
2. Search a real park by name and show distance-based results.
3. Open a liked park and show check-in, park chat, and live activity.
4. Show a dog profile with private document storage and expiry dates.
5. Show walk tracking or saved walk history.
6. Show groups, direct messages, and local pet services.
