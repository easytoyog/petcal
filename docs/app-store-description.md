## Full App Store Description
In the Park is the all-in-one app for dog owners who want to stay active, organized, and connected.

Search parks by name, save your favorite parks, track walks, manage pet records, and discover local dog life in one place.

Whether you are heading out for a quick walk, planning your weekly routine, organizing your dog’s important records, or exploring new places to go, In the Park helps make dog ownership simpler.

### Why dog owners love In the Park
- Search parks and dog-friendly spots faster
- Track walks and build healthier routines
- Keep dog records and documents organized
- Stay connected with local chats and groups
- Discover local dog services and community

### Features
- Search parks by name and sort by distance
- Save liked parks for quick access
- Identify dog parks and off-leash park options
- Check in at parks and see live activity
- Join park chat and group chat
- Send direct messages
- Track walks and review walk history
- Create profiles for multiple dogs
- Store pet documents with expiry dates
- Manage vaccine, licence, microchip, insurance, medical, and custom records
- Browse dog-owner news and tips
- Discover local pet services

### Built for real dog-owner routines
In the Park is designed around what dog owners actually do every week:
- find a park
- go for a walk
- stay organized
- keep records ready
- connect with local dog life

That makes it useful even if you want practical tools first and community second.

### Great for
- dog owners who visit parks often
- new puppy parents building routines
- multi-dog households
- people who want pet records organized in one place
- anyone looking for a smarter dog-owner app

Download In the Park and make your dog’s daily routine easier to manage.
