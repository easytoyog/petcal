# In the Park Marketing Kit

## Best Positioning Right Now
The strongest message today is not “social network for dog owners.”

It is:

**The all-in-one app for dog owners to find parks, track walks, manage pet records, and discover local dog life.**

That message works whether the user is looking for:
- a park
- a walk tracker
- dog records and documents
- local community
- pet services

## Core Message Pillars
- Search parks by name and save liked parks
- Track walks and build active routines
- Store dog documents with expiry dates
- Join park chat, group chat, and direct messages
- Discover local pet services
- Browse dog-owner news and tips in the app

## Primary Headline Recommendations
- Find parks. Track walks. Manage dog life.
- The all-in-one app for dog owners
- Parks, walks, records, and local dog community
- One app for your dog’s parks, walks, and records
- Built for dog owners on the go

## Fast Demo Flow
### 20-second demo
1. Show the `For You` tab
2. Search a park by name
3. Show liked parks
4. Show a dog profile with documents
5. Mention walk tracking and local chats

### 45-second demo
1. Open `For You`
2. Search a local park and show distance-based results
3. Open a dog profile and show private document storage with expiry dates
4. Show walk tracking or walk history
5. Show park chat, groups, or DMs
6. End with: “It helps dog owners stay active, organized, and connected in one app.”
